Embedded Illusion

This optical illusion is an illusion that imprints itself into your eyes.
Meaning after staring for a bit, you see the illusion pattern everytime you blink.
This is because there is a "neural delay" of about 100 milliseconds between when a scene
enters your eyes and when your brain perceives it. So even after you have already recieved the
image your brain still processes it as if it is still there very faintly.
